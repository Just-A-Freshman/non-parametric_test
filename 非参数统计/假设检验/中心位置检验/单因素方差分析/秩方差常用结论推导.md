## 1. 符号说明
- $k$为组数, $N$为总样本量
- $\sum\limits_{i=1}^k \sum\limits_{j=1}^{n_i}$简化成$\sum\limits_{ij}$
- $\sum\limits_{j=1}^{n_i}$简化成$\sum\limits_j$，$\sum\limits_{i=1}^k$简化成$\sum\limits_i$

## 2. SST、SSE和SSA
- $SST$：直接展开
$$\begin{aligned}
SST &= \sum_{ij} (R_{ij} - \bar{R})^2 \\
&= \sum_{ij} (R_{ij}^2 - 2R_{ij}\bar{R} + \bar{R}^2) \\
&= \sum_{ij} R_{ij}^2 - 2\bar{R} \sum_{ij} R_{ij} + \sum_{ij} \bar{R}^2 \\
&= \sum_{ij} R_{ij}^2 - N \bar{R}^2\\
&= \frac{N(1+N)(2N+1)}{6} - N \left[\frac{(1+N)N}{2}\right]^2 \\
&= \frac{N(N+1)(N-1)}{12}
\end{aligned}$$

- $SSE$：直接展开
$$\begin{aligned}
SSE &= \sum_{ij} (R_{ij} - \bar{R_{i.}})^2 \\
&= \sum_{ij} (R_{ij}^2 - 2R_{ij}\bar{R_{i.}} + \bar{R_{i.}}^2) \\
&= \sum_{ij} R_{ij}^2 - 2 \sum_i \bar{R_{i.}}\sum_j R_{ij} + \sum_i n_i \bar{R_{i.}}^2 \\
&= \sum_{ij} R_{ij}^2 - \sum_i n_i \bar{R_{i.}}^2 \\
&= \frac{N(1+N)(2N+1)}{6} - \sum_{i=1}^k n_i \bar{R_{i.}}^2
\end{aligned}$$

- $SSA$：利用关系式$SSA = SST - SSE$可得：
$$
SSA = \sum_{i=1}^k n_i \bar{R_{i.}}^2 - \frac{N(N+1)^2}{4}
$$

## 3. $\bar{R_i}$相关性质
- 期望
$$\begin{aligned}
E(\bar{R_i}) &= E(\frac{1}{n_i} \sum_{j=1}^{n_i} R_{ij}) \\
&= \frac{1}{n_i} \sum_{j=1}^{n_i} E(R_{ij}) \\
&= \frac{1}{n_i} \cdot n_i \cdot \frac{N + 1}{2} \\
&= \frac{N + 1}{2}
\end{aligned}$$

- 方差
$$\begin{aligned}
Var(\bar{R_i}) &= Var(\frac{1}{n_i} \sum_{j=1}^{n_i} R_{ij}) \\
&= \frac{1}{n_i^2} Var(\sum_{j=1}^{n_i} R_{ij}) \\
&= \frac{1}{n_i^2} \left[\sum_{j=1}^{n_i} Var(R_{ij}) + 2\sum_{a \neq b}Cov(R_{ia}, R_{ib}) \right] \\
&= \frac{1}{n_i^2} \left[n_i \cdot \frac{n^2 - 1}{12} + 2 \binom{n_i}{2} \left(-\frac{n + 1}{12}\right)\right] \\
&= \frac{(n + 1)(n - n_i)}{12}
\end{aligned}$$
